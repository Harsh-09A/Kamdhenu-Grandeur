Welcome to Kamdhenu Grandeur

Kamdhenu Grandeur is an aesthetic residential address in the prime neighbourhood of Sanpada in Navi Mumbai. The edifice presents lavish 2, 3 and 4 BHK cloudcrest apartments. The finely designed apartments offer optimum spaces, immense ventilation and astounding views of the Parsik Hill and Arabian Sea. Every residence is equipped with elegant interiors, branded fittings and wide sun decks. The highest floor-to-ceiling height gives a palatial feel. This stunning community comprises amenities like a 5-level podium parking, landscaped garden, gymnasium, kids play area, swimming pool, senior citizen area, indoor games and more.

Ryan International School, Jaipuriar School, Sadhu Vaswani International School, New Millennium Multispeciality Hospital, Fortis Hospital, Inorbit Mall, Raghuleela Mall, Dmart and Zudio are within a few minutes from the property. The Palm Beach Road provides quick access to prime commercial areas of Navi Mumbai. The Sion Panvel Highway, Thane Belapur Road and Sanpada Railway Station offer broader connectivity towards Mumbai, Pune and Thane. So, acquire an affluent residence with mesmerising views at Kamdhenu Grandeur in Sanpada.

About Kamdhenu Lifespaces

Founded in 1955, Kamdhenu Realities is a leading real estate company in Navi Mumbai. The firm develops world-class residential, commercial, and retail properties in locales such as Ulwe, Sanpada, Taloja, & Kharghar. Some of its top properties are The Landmark and Eden Garden in Kharghar.

According to the RERA Act 2016 of the Government of India, all projects of the Kamdhenu Lifespaces including the Kamdhenu Grandeur, are listed on the Maharashtra Government's RERA website under registered projects.
